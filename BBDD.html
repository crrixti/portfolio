PPS-SGP, or the Small Grants Program, is one of the United Nations (UN) groups dedicated to the development of local population welfare and environmental actions in the Dominican Republic. They currently work together on all their projects with Guakía Ambiente, a civil society organization that emerged as an alternative for territorial action.
The actions of PPS and Guakía Ambiente operate on different scales across the national territory, basing their projects on the economic and social development of rural communities through the following three strategic pillars: promotion of sustainable livelihoods, environmental research and studies, and the building of capacities and institutional strengthening of the communities themselves.
